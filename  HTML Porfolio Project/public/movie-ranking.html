The Best Movie According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-times.

Black Hawk Down

"Black Hawk Down" is appreciated by audiences for its gripping storytelling, powerful performances, and its unflinching portrayal of the realities of war.

Men Of Honor

"Men of Honor" is a moving and memorable film that highlights the power of resilience and the human spirit.

Black Panther

The film celebrates African culture and showcases a predominantly black cast. It offers representation and visibility to underrepresented groups in mainstream media, fostering a sense of pride and empowerment among audiences.
